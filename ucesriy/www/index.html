person_pin_circleTrack my location

Overview

This application is an andriod based application.
To install the application, please scan QR code below.
It will track your location, and pop up quiz question nearby for you to answer

Step 1 - Download application and Set up

After scanning QR code, there would be link to download APK file
Open the link. Then, press "download" to download APK file.
Open APK file and press "Install"
**Noted that you should enable the location service on your phone**

Step 2 - Track Your Location

When launching a Quiz App
press person_pin_circleTrack my location button to track your location

Step 3 - Answer the Quiz

If there is question nearby, the quiz's location, quiz and choice will pop up
Fill in your userID and click on the radio button to answer the quiz

Step 4 - Upload your answer

Upload your answer by pressing publishAnswer button
Press "OK" to start upload.
The alert message would pop up saying your answer is "correct" or "wrong"
If the answer is wrong, it would show the correct choice for you.
